Bangladesh ENT Hospital Ltd

A premier specialized hospital dedicated exclusively to Ear, Nose, and Throat care in Bangladesh. We are committed to providing world-class ENT treatments with compassion and cutting-edge technology.

Established in 2005, Bangladesh ENT Hospital has been at the forefront of ENT care in the region. Our journey began with a vision to create a specialized center that would provide comprehensive ENT services under one roof.

Over the years, we have grown to become the largest and most advanced ENT hospital in Bangladesh, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by highly qualified specialists.

Our hospital follows international standards of healthcare while understanding the unique needs of our local patients. We believe in a patient-centric approach where every individual receives personalized care and attention.

Our Journey

Milestones in our path to becoming Bangladesh's leading ENT hospital

2005

Hospital Foundation

Bangladesh ENT Hospital Ltd was established with 50 beds and 5 specialist doctors, becoming the first specialized ENT hospital in the country.

2008

Advanced Surgical Wing

Opened our state-of-the-art surgical wing with 3 modern operation theaters equipped with endoscopic and microscopic facilities.

2012

Audiology Center

Launched the Advanced Audiology and Hearing Aid Center, providing comprehensive hearing care services.

2015

Expansion & Modernization

Expanded to 150 beds and introduced advanced diagnostic facilities including CT scan and digital X-ray for ENT.

2018

Sleep Lab & Allergy Center

Established dedicated Sleep Laboratory and Allergy & Immunology Center for comprehensive ENT care.

2022

Robotic Surgery & Telemedicine

Introduced robotic-assisted ENT surgery and launched telemedicine services for remote consultations.
